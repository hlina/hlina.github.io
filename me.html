Lina Huang is currently a Masters and Bachelors candidate in Systems Science and Engineering with a Computer Science minor at the University of Pennsylvania. Her main academic interests are agent-based modeling and stimulations, and data analysis. She has conducted independent research using statistics to analyze survey results about effective advertising across age groups. Most recently, she has used agent-based modeling through AnyLogic to model the hospitalization system for mental health patient in order to analyze factors affecting readmission rates. These results were published in a book chapter called “Artificial Intelligence and Human Behavior Modeling: Simulation for Mental Health Conditions.”

As a student , she has interned at PricewaterhouseCoopers as a technology consultant, and SingTel as a DataSpark analyst. For more information about these internships, please see the resume section.
